How it works

Three steps.
One honest score.

01

Verify, anonymously

Confirm you worked there with a payslip or work email — then we strip every identifier. Your review can never be traced back to you.

02

Rate honestly

Six axes: management, hours, comp, growth, culture, ethics. One paragraph each. Required. No vague star-reviews.

03

Public score, forever

We aggregate every verified review into one public score per company. Companies don't pay to suppress them. Ever.

Principles

Workers first.
Recruiters first.
Employer branding.

Companies can't pay to hide

No "premium employer profile" tier. Every score is public. Permanent.

Verified or it doesn't count

Every review is verified. No anonymous trolling, no astroturfing.

Identity strip, on submit

We verify, then forget. Reviews are unlinkable to your account by design.

Free for workers, paid by truth

Reading and reviewing is free. Companies pay only for verified hiring data — never to edit scores.
