Ad Disclosure

Tomato Clock

This Advertising Disclosure also applies to Screen Lab.

Tomato Clock and Screen Lab use a dedicated reward page to show advertising that can unlock premium functionality for a limited time.

How Ads Work

  • When a user chooses to unlock a premium feature, the extension may open a dedicated reward page on novato.dev.
  • That page may load advertising content served by Adcash.
  • After the reward flow is completed, the page returns the user to the extension to finish the temporary unlock.

Third-Party Advertising

Advertising on the reward page may be served by Adcash or its partners. Those providers may process device, browser, IP, or engagement data according to their own policies.

No Purchase Required

Watching advertising is used as an optional way to unlock premium functionality temporarily. Users can continue using the base features without engaging with advertisements.
